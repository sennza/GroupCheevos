Group Achievements for BuddyPress

Group Achievements for BuddyPress is a plugin to enable your Achievements plugin to have achievements at the group level.

For example, you may want to only give members their achievement karma when all members of their group have unlocked the achievement.

Requirements

  • Achievements
  • WordPress 3.5 or newer

Installation

  • Download and install the Achievements plugin from the WordPress.org repository.
  • Install this plugin
